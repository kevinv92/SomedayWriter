# Church of St. Monica

A church in the Edgware Road where, on a bright autumn morning, @{Godfrey Norton}
marries @{Irene Adler} in a hurried ceremony — and @{Sherlock Holmes}, lounging in
by chance, finds himself the legally required witness.
