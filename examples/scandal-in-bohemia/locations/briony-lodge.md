# Briony Lodge

A bijou villa in Serpentine Avenue, @{Irene Adler}'s home. A pretty house with a
garden at the back, a sitting-room to the front — and, hidden behind a sliding
panel beside the bell-pull, @{The Photograph} the King would give a province to
recover.
