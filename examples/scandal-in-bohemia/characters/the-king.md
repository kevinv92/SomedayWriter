# Wilhelm von Ormstein

Wilhelm Gottsreich Sigismond von Ormstein, Grand Duke of Cassel-Felstein and
hereditary King of Bohemia. Six-foot-six, wrapped in astrakhan and a black
half-mask, he calls at @{Baker Street} under the thin alias **Count Von Kramm**.

He is about to marry into Scandinavian royalty, and one indiscreet photograph
stands to ruin the match.

## Traits

- Wears his rank like armour and hides it badly.
- Wronged @{Irene Adler}; now fears her more than any army.
- Offers @{The Emerald Ring} as a fee, and is refused.
