# Godfrey Norton

A dashing young barrister of @{The Temple}, and a frequent caller at
@{Briony Lodge}. He courts @{Irene Adler} at speed and, in the space of a single
morning, marries her at the @{Church of St. Monica} — with a startled
@{Sherlock Holmes} pressed into service as witness.

## Traits

- Handsome, hot-tempered, and quick to the altar.
- Loves @{Irene}; asks no questions about her past.
