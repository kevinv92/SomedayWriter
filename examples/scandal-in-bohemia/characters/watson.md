# John Watson

Doctor, veteran, and chronicler. Newly married and returned to civil practice,
he is drawn back into @{Baker Street} the moment @{Holmes} needs a witness — and
a pair of steady hands to throw a plumber's rocket through a window.

## Traits

- Loyal, observant, and reliably astonished.
- Narrates; misses the obvious until it is explained.
