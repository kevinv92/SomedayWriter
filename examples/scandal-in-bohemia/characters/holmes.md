# Sherlock Holmes

The world's first consulting detective, of @{Baker Street}. A reasoning and
observing machine — cold, precise, and admirably balanced — who claims never to
guess and to see what others merely look at.

In this case he is out-thought exactly once, and remembers it forever after.

## Traits

- Deduces a man's history from his boots and his cuffs.
- Loves a disguise; passes as a drunken groom or an amiable clergyman at will.
- Keeps the fee he asks for in a place of honour.
