# Irene Adler

Contralto, adventuress, and the one antagonist to beat @{Sherlock Holmes} at his
own game. Born in New Jersey, retired from the operatic stage, and living quietly
at @{Briony Lodge} — until a King's clumsiness makes her dangerous.

She has the face of the most beautiful of women and the mind of the most
resolute of men. To @{Holmes} she is, ever after, simply **the woman**.

## Traits

- Sees through a disguise faster than the detective wears it out.
- Keeps @{The Photograph} as insurance, not as spite.
- Loves once, marries fast, and vanishes clean.
