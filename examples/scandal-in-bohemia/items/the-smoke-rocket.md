# The Smoke Rocket

A plumber's smoke-rocket — a cardboard tube that vents dense white smoke and a
cry of "Fire!" @{Holmes} has @{Watson} pitch it through the sitting-room window of
@{Briony Lodge} so that @{Irene Adler}, in her panic, will bolt straight to the
thing she values most and betray its hiding place.
